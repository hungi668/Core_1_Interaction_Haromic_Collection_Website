The Frick Collection

Overview

The Frick Collection is an intimate collection of European art from the 14th to 19th centuries. It opened in 1935 by industrialist Henry Clay Frick. It recently renovated and reopened its second floor in 2025. Their museum in unique for its domestic scale with rooms once lived by the Frick family themselves, adorned with fine furniture and a tranquil atmosphere. This refined setting offers a contemplative and immersive version of art history.
