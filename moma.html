Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)

Overview

The Museum of Modern Art is a respected institution dedicated to contemporary art. Their collection includes works from Van Gogh, Picasso, Monet, Dalí, Warhol, and more. MoMA also offers special rotating exhibitions, educational programs, and interactive media arts. Whether you are a casual art viewer or artist yourself, the MoMA is a great place to see how art has shaped the modern world.
