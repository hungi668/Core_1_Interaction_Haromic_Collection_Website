Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum

Overview

Cooper Hewitt, Smithsonian Design Museum is a historical and contemporary collection of words at the Andrew Carnegie Mansion. This collection includes furniture, wallpaper, textiles, decorative arts, and digital design. There are interactive tables, immersive installations, and hands-on workshops. The architecture and rich interiors shape the museum experience.
