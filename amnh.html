American Museum of Natural History (AMNH)

Overview

The American Museum of Natural History is an interactive learning exhibit of a vast collection of natural science artifacts. Their exhibits range from tiny insects to visualizations of galaxies and intergalactic space exploration. Not to mention the dinosaur fossils, wild animals, gems, minerals, cultural items, planetarium shows, and seasonal exhibits. AMNH is not just a museum, but also a research center for discovery and conservation. They inspire future generations to learn about biodiversity and storytelling of nature.
