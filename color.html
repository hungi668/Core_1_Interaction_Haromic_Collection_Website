Color Factory NYC

Overview

Color Factory NYC is a multi-sensory experience designed for fun, engagement, vibrancy, and playfulness. Each room offers surprises such as reflections, confetti, treats, photo-opportunities, and family-friendly moments. Curiosity is stretch to new lengths as visitors fo through each sensory experience, a stark contrast to more formal museums.
