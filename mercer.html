Mercer Labs, Museum of Art & Technology

Overview

Mercer Labs, Museum of Art & Technology, transforms a gallery into an interactive and immersive sensory experience where art meets innovation. Each room is paired with a unique combination of light, sound, projection, and sculptures. Visitors experience the art rather than merely looking at it, bridging gaps between art and innovation.
