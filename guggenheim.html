Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum

Overview

TThe Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum is famous for its unique architecture and collection of artworks. The building was designed by Frank Lloyd Wright, featuring a spiraling rotunda that has visitors going in circles. Galleries and viewing spaces flow into one another, making it a smooth viewing experience. The thoughtfully created exhibits highlighting Impressionism, mid-century, and contemporary art.
