The Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met)

Overview

The Metropolitan Museum of Art hosts more than 2 million works, spanning thousands of years of human evolution and history around the world. There is a great diversity of exhibits such as European paintings, costumes, textiles, ancient artifacts, among others. The building has become an icon in itself. The famous steps adorning the entrance with its grand facade has been featured in numerous TV shows.
